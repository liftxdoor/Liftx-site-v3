LiftX Warranty

Workmanship backed by LiftX.

Qualifying repairs and installations include a 1-year workmanship warranty with three scheduled maintenance visits during the warranty period.

Coverage Overview

Clear coverage. Clear expectations.

1-Year Workmanship Coverage

Covers qualifying installation and repair workmanship performed directly by LiftX Door Systems.

Three Maintenance Visits

Scheduled approximately every four months during the warranty period for qualifying work.

Manufacturer Coverage

Eligible parts, doors, and operators may also include separate manufacturer warranties.

Important exclusions

Coverage may be voided by unauthorized repairs, service performed by another company or maintenance crew, misuse, impact damage, framing or structural problems, weather-related damage, improper maintenance, or conditions outside LiftX control. Consumable components and normal wear are not covered unless specifically stated.
