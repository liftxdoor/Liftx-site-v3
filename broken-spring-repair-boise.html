Common Questions

What customers ask before service.

Can I open the door with a broken spring?

A door with a broken spring can be extremely heavy and unsafe to lift. Avoid repeatedly running the opener because that can cause additional damage.

Should both springs be replaced together?

On a two-spring system, replacing both is often the practical option when they are the same age and cycle history. The correct recommendation depends on the system condition.

How do I know the correct spring size?

Spring size is determined from the door weight, dimensions, track configuration, drums, and spring system—not by guessing from appearance alone.
