Common Questions

What customers ask before service.

Can LiftX repair a door that will not open?

Yes. Common causes include broken springs, cable problems, opener failures, locked controls, track damage, or an unbalanced door. A proper inspection determines the actual cause.

Should I repair or replace my garage door?

That depends on the condition of the sections, track, hardware, springs, and operator. LiftX explains whether a practical repair makes sense before recommending replacement.

Can I text pictures before scheduling?

Yes. Photos of the inside door system, damaged area, springs, and opener can help LiftX understand the setup before arriving.
