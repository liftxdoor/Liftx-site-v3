Common Questions

What customers ask before service.

What qualifies as an emergency garage door repair?

A trapped vehicle, unsecured opening, door off track, broken spring, hanging section, or failed commercial access point may require urgent service.

Should I keep operating a damaged door?

No. Repeated operation can worsen damage or create a safety risk. Stop using the door and contact a qualified technician.

Can I text LiftX a video or photos?

Yes. Photos or a short video can show the door position, damaged hardware, opener behavior, and whether the opening is secure.
